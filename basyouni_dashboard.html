Flag Class D individuals for health interventions
This group averages 27.7% body fat and 72 kg — significantly higher than all other classes. Structured weight management with endurance training is the priority prescription.
Critical
3
Reconsider gender-biased fitness programming
Women represent 44.3% of Class A despite being only 36.8% of the dataset. Rebalancing toward flexibility and endurance metrics better recognizes female performance patterns.
Strategic
4
Deploy Random Forest as a screening tool
At 73.9% accuracy (CV 72%) the model is stable for initial fitness screening, especially Class D identification. Use as a triage filter — not a final diagnosis.
